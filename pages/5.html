The archive contains one final document from the original research team. It was found sealed in a fireproof box, buried beneath the foundation of their abandoned facility.
Dated the day before they disappeared. Written in Dr. Aris's handwriting, but with noticeable tremors not present in earlier notes.
It doesn't describe observations. It doesn't analyze data. It contains only a single, repeated sentence, written forty-seven times.
Each iteration slightly different. As if trying to correct a translation error. Or communicate something that language can't properly contain.
"It wasn't observing us. We were observing it observing itself through us. The silence isn't empty. The silence is the observer."
